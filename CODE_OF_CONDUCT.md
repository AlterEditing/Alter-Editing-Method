# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification first.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or intimidation
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues/PRs

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this policy.
Severe or repeated violations can result in temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
